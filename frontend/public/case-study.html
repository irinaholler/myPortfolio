Known Limitations & Safeguards

Realistic boundaries and responsible design choices

No answers outside indexed documents

The AI system only responds using information from verified restaurant documents stored in Pinecone. This prevents hallucination and ensures accuracy, but means it cannot answer questions about topics not in the knowledge base.

Ambiguous questions trigger clarification

When a question is unclear or could have multiple interpretations, the system asks for clarification rather than guessing. This maintains quality but may require an extra interaction.

Lead capture only activates on explicit intent

Contact information is only captured when the system detects clear booking intent. Casual mentions of names or emails in conversation are not stored, protecting user privacy.
